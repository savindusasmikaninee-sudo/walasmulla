# Culture Page Additions Summary

## Updates Made to culture.html

### 1. Photo Gallery Section (20 Photos)
A new photo gallery section has been added showcasing cultural moments from Walasmulla.

**Features:**
- 20 gallery images in a responsive grid layout
- Hover effects with zoom and overlay
- Images cover various cultural aspects:
  - Temple festival processions
  - Traditional dancers and drummers
  - Community gatherings
  - Vesak decorations
  - New Year celebrations
  - Religious ceremonies
  - Cultural performances
  - Traditional crafts

**Image Files Required:**
- `images/gallery-1.jpg` through `images/gallery-20.jpg`
- Recommended size: 800x600px or similar 4:3 ratio
- Images should depict various cultural activities and celebrations

### 2. Community Testimonials Section (4 Comments)
A testimonials section featuring voices from the Walasmulla community.

**Featured Community Members:**

1. **Nimal Jayasuriya** - Village Elder & Temple Committee Member
   - Talks about how Katina Perahera brings the community together
   - Emphasizes family reunions during festivals

2. **Chandima Perera** - Primary School Teacher
   - Discusses children's participation in cultural activities
   - Highlights the balance between tradition and modern education

3. **Sunil Fernando** - Local Business Owner
   - Describes the incredible sense of community
   - Explains how neighbors work together during celebrations

4. **Dilani Rathnayake** - IT Professional & Former Resident
   - Shares perspective as someone who works in Colombo
   - Expresses why Walasmulla will always be home

**Design Features:**
- Elegant card-based layout
- Avatar icons for each person (emoji-based)
- Quote marks as decorative elements
- Hover animations
- Responsive grid that adapts to screen size

## Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (> 768px)
- Gallery: 4-5 columns
- Testimonials: 2-3 columns side by side

### Tablet (768px)
- Gallery: 2-3 columns
- Testimonials: 1-2 columns

### Mobile (< 480px)
- Gallery: 2 columns
- Testimonials: 1 column (stacked)
